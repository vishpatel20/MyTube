Nicktoons: Attack of the ToyBots

This is the second sequel of the nicktoons series and in this one, a bunch of robots try to take over from an evil scientist in Jimmy's universe and once again, all the toons have to come together to stop him. This game was more interactive and challenging than the sequels but loads of fun and memories were made trying to complete it.
